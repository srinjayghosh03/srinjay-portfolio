Who Am I?

I’m a fourth-year B.Tech student at KIIT with a passion for programming, web development, and building digital experiences that matter. I specialize in Python, Java, and full-stack web development, creating clean, intuitive, and dynamic applications.

My Journey Through Code

From interning at my university’s digital team to consulting at Shine Projects, my experience spans digital media, marketing, and development. I thrive where logic meets design, using code to communicate, solve, and inspire.

A Lens on the World

Photography is my way of freezing time. From sunrises in the mountains to vibrant city streets, I capture the world as I see it—vivid, emotional, and alive. My camera is my second language, expressing what words often can’t.

Why I Travel

Travel is more than a passion—it’s my purpose. It grounds me, inspires me, and fuels my creativity. Mountains, especially, are my soul’s home. One day, I dream of building tech remotely from a cabin in the hills.

Vision Forward

I’m working toward a future where I can bridge technology, design, and storytelling. Whether it’s a startup, a digital agency, or my own venture—I aim to create meaningful digital experiences that help people connect, discover, and dream.
