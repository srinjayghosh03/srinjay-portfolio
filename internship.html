My Internship Journey

My Internship Experiences

📡 KIIT University Digital Team

At KIIT, I had the incredible opportunity to intern with the university’s official digital media team. My role was at the crossroads of creativity and communication — handling the university’s social media platforms, assisting with live event coverage, and contributing to the university website’s design and updates.

I worked behind the scenes on major events, ensuring timely posts and engaging content, helping increase our reach and interaction by a significant margin. Through tools like Google Suite and CMS platforms, I helped streamline the digital workflow, wrote compelling captions, edited event photos, and assisted in real-time broadcasting for larger campus events.

This experience taught me how impactful clear digital storytelling can be — and how much work goes into making it seamless.

💼 Shine Projects – Marketing Consultant Intern

At Shine Projects, a dynamic Hyderabad-based startup, I took on the role of a Marketing Consultant Intern for two months. Here, I explored the real-world impact of strategic digital outreach. My work involved generating leads, understanding customer personas, and promoting career-oriented courses through a combination of email marketing, LinkedIn campaigns, and cold outreach strategies.

I contributed to expanding our active user base by using creative messaging and tailored communications. I also assisted in data collection and analysis to help refine targeting and improve conversions — gaining insights into what truly drives engagement in the digital learning space.

This internship sharpened my marketing instincts and taught me the power of customer-centric design in campaigns.
